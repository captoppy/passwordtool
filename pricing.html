Common questions
Is this really one-time? No subscription?

Yes. You buy a pack, get your reviews, done. No recurring charges, no cancellation drama. We'll introduce subscriptions later for power users, but that's not the model right now.

How long until my reviews come in?

Once your project is live on the Ladder, reviews typically come in within 48–72 hours. Larger packs may take a few days longer depending on tester activity.

What makes a review "verified"?

Every review requires a minimum 3-minute dwell time on your project, a star rating, at least one friction point or magic moment, and a written summary. Thin or copy-paste reviews are flagged and don't count toward your pack.

Can I buy more reviews after my pack runs out?

Yes. Buy another pack at any time. Each new pack refreshes your listing window and can upgrade your Ladder position.

What currency will I be charged in?

Stripe charges in USD. The currency switcher above is indicative — your bank applies conversion at checkout. Actual charge is in USD.

What's the free path — is it really free?

Complete 5 approved missions as a tester and you can list your own project for free (base position, 5 reviews, 7 days). It's real, but it's limited intentionally. Paid packs always rank above free listings.

What is the Jellar Monthly newsletter?

A regular newsletter sent to our full builder and tester community. Sponsored slot buyers get their project featured in the next issue — name, link, and a short description. Great for extra reach beyond the Ladder.

When do payments go live?

Stripe Checkout activates at launch. During the MVP period, email us to reserve your founding price — we'll lock it in and invoice when Stripe is live.
