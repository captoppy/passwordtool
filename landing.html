For Builders

Real testers who can do the actual thing

Jellar testers are builders themselves — they use the same tools you do. If your app requires a login, they have an account. If it uses OAuth, they can connect it.

GitHub OAuth

Testers can log in with GitHub. Common for dev tools, dashboards, and API products.

Google OAuth

Google sign-in works. Testers can connect Google accounts where required.

Twitter / X OAuth

Social login with X. Testers can authorise apps that require a connected X account.

Email sign-up

Testers use test credentials — not their real email — when your app requires a new account sign-up.

Free plan features

Testers can explore and test free tiers of your product. Paid-only features should be noted in your mission brief.

Web apps only

Jellar currently tests web-based projects only. iOS and Android native app testing is on the roadmap.

One rule testers always follow: they never enter real financial information or sensitive personal data into test projects. Design your mission brief with this in mind.
What your feedback looks like
